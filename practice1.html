People with disabilities face a variety of challenges. They must deal with many issues and obstacles in order to perform their daily lives properly. However, quick technological advancements have aided those with disabilities as well. By utilizing computing systems for skills such as reading, writing, communication, walking, and many others, technology is assisting in the reduction of barriers. Technology has given disabled people the ability to conduct a wide range of daily activities independently, but they still face a number of challenges when it comes to computer use.
